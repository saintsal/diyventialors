How we work

  • Facilitated. We personally connect you to the right answers and people, to set you up fast and move you forward.
  • In the open. Daily updates, open schematics. That means faster help and less reinventing wheels.
  • Independent, parallel teams. No one device will work everywhere, so we work on a range of solutions.
  • No need for permission. This is a distributed community. Do what you think is needed.

Already prototyping?

We want to give you what you need, the easiest way possible.

  1. If you need something quick, email us. or jump into the chatroom.
  2. Or, fill out this 1-minute form so we can get working for you.

We will:

  • Get you answers
  • Connect you to whoever you need
  • Set up a place where helpers can find you
  • Inform you when another team does something useful for you

This project run 100% by volunteers and is non-profit. It is a collaboration between Source Institute - a peer learning skunkworks, Sofia Tech Park - Bulgaria's largest science park, and BESCO - The Bulgarian Startup Association. It is spearheaded by Salim Virani and Nato Stefanov (also volunteering), with support from many other volunteers who've just stepped up in a time of need.

We are doing this to help people in all countries, but basing out of our resources here in Bulgaria for fast action.

Contact us in the chat room (for quick things) or the forum.
