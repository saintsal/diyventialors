DIY Ventilators

An open library for sharing information between independent, self-directed respirator teams.

For those who believe in open, parallel collaboration and prioritising speed to functional respiration devices above all else. Our approach is anti-fragile.

Here you'll find open lists on designs, support, supplies, funding, as well as reference information for engineers collected from doctors answering engineers.

What should I work on?

We are focusing on supporting teams and individuals who are proactive in building prototypes. We recommend you start working independently on one of the progressed designs and share your progress sharing daily updates with other teams.

You can direct yourself by first reading the latest daily update and understanding our strategy.

Please ask for any clarification or support you need on our forum.

This project is a collaboration between Source Institute - a peer learning skunkworks, Sofia Tech Park - Bulgaria's largest science park, and BESCO - The Bulgarian Startup Association. It is spearheaded by Salim Virani and Nato Stefanov, with support from many other volunteers who've just stepped up in a time of need.

We are doing this to help people in all countries, but basing out of our resources here in Bulgaria for fast action.
