DIY Ventilators

This is an open library for spreading actionable information between independent, self-directed teams that are building resperators as fast as possible.

Our approach is open, parallel, and anti-fragile. We priortise speed to functional respiration devices above all else.

Below you'll find open lists on designs, support, supplies, funding, as well as reference information for engineers collected from doctors answering engineers.

Getting involved

Sign Up

Adding yourself to the list helps people find you to help, and you'll also get daily email updates with resources and requests for help. It takes 1 minute.

Get support for your project

So facilitators and connectors can start working on your behalf to connect you to the resources we've mobilised, like supplies, funding, access to hospital testing, etc.

What should I work on?

We are focusing on supporting teams and individuals who are proactive in building prototypes. You can direct yourself by first reading the latest daily update and understanding our strategy, and please ask for any clarification or support you need.

This project is a collaboration between Source Institute - a peer learning skunkworks, Sofia Tech Park - Bulgaria's largest science park, and BESCO - The Bulgarian Startup Association. It is spearheaded by Salim Virani and Nato Stefanov, with support from many other volunteers who've just stepped up in a time of need.

We are doing this to help people in all countries, but basing out of our resources here in Bulgaria for fast action.
